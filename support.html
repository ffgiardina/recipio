Frequently asked

My recipes disappeared after reinstalling the app.
Rezeptli stores recipes locally on your device. If iCloud sync wasn't enabled before you deleted the app, that data isn't recoverable. Enabling iCloud sync in Settings keeps a copy in your own iCloud account.

How do I import a recipe from YouTube?
From the "New Recipe" screen, choose "Import from YouTube" and paste the video link. Rezeptli reads the video's captions to build the recipe.

How do I export or share a recipe?
Open a recipe and use the share icon to export it as a .rezeptli file or generate a shareable link.
